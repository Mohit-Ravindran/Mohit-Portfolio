MY STORY

Beyond the Interface: The Untold Story of My Design Passion.

A mechanical engineer by education, my journey into the world of design began as I explored career paths post-graduation. Drawn to the realm of digital creativity, I found my footing in graphic design. As I delved deeper, the allure of UI/UX design captivated me—it was more than pixels; it was about crafting seamless user experiences.

Opening my laptop on Monday mornings transformed from a routine into a canvas for my creative expression. The joy I found in design erased boredom and stress, affirming that this was my calling. With a naturally creative mindset, I'm on a perpetual learning journey in UI/UX design. Why? Because the pursuit of knowledge is a lifelong adventure, and I'm here to design my way through it."

Career Milestones

With over 4 years of design expertise, my journey has been enriched by collaborations with major industry players such as Mantralabs, Codegama, Ninjaas Labs, Blueprints, and Powerica. Throughout this trajectory, I've not only showcased my design prowess but also played a pivotal role in elevating the visual and functional aspects of numerous apps and websites.

From the early days to reaching a senior design level, my passion for creating intuitive and visually stunning digital experiences has been a driving force. With each project, I've honed my skills, leaving an indelible mark on the design landscape. As I continue to evolve, my commitment to pushing the boundaries of creativity remains unwavering, ensuring that every design reflects not just experience but innovation.
